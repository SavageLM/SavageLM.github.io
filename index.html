About Me

For the better part of the last decade, I have worked in the Aerospace Industry as a Non-Destructive Inspector specializing in Ultrasonics.

With my interest in computers and robotics, as well as my background in NDT, I decided to pursue systems programming at Atlas to further my career.

As a systems programmer, I have a strong interest in intergrating hardware-level and software-level programming design emphasizing optimization and efficiency while maintaining clear, concise code.

I'm a life-long learner who enjoys puzzles and am comfortable working both solo and as a member of a team.

Between my hard work ethic and tendency to strive for perfection, I pride my myself on often going above and beyond.

Currently interested in and studying to improve knowledge of C++ as well as to obtain skills necessary to develop for embedded systems.
